About

Om Pranab Mohanty

I do not write by habit, only by necessity. Most of what appears here arrives without warning and leaves before it can be shaped.

These pieces were not meant to be finished, revised, or explained. Some were written in margins, some behind other thoughts, some before I understood what they were asking for.

What lives here is unfiltered, unpolished, and unashamed. These are the thoughts that refused to stay quiet.
